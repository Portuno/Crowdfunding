Frequently asked questions.

Que es el crowdfunding?

El crowdfunding es un modelo de financiación colectiva que permite a individuos o empresas recaudar fondos para proyectos, productos o causas a través de la contribución de un gran número de personas, generalmente a través de plataformas en linea especializadas.

What are the most common types of crowdfunding?

The most common types of crowdfunding are:
Donations: People contribute to a cause without expecting a tangible reward.
Rewards: Contributors receive rewards or products in exchange for their financial support.

What are the benefits of crowdfunding?

Some benefits of crowdfunding include:
Access to funding without relying on traditional financial institutions.
Validation of interest in a project before its launch.
Opportunity to involve the community in the development process.
Potential to create connections and build a follower base.

What are the risks associated with crowdfunding?

Failing to reach the funding goal and losing time and resources.
Legal or intellectual property problems if not managed properly.
Responsibility towards backers to fulfill promises of rewards or products.
Possible unfair competition or idea copying by others.

What happens if my crowdfunding campaign doesn't reach its funding goal?

If your campaign doesn't reach its funding goal before the deadline, typically no charges are made to sponsors, and no funds are received. It's important to have a backup plan in case this happens, such as seeking alternative funding sources or adjusting the campaign strategy.

How can I ensure that my crowdfunding campaign is successful?

To increase the chances of success for your crowdfunding campaign, it is important to:
Research and plan your project thoroughly.
Set realistic and transparent funding goals.
Create an outgoing marketing strategy to promote your campaign.
Maintain clear and consistent communication with your followers and sponsors.
